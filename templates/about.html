Purpose

The NCERT Book Chatbot is designed to help students of classes 9 to 12 access information and study materials from NCERT textbooks. It aims to provide a convenient way for students to get answers to their questions and understand complex topics easily.

Features

  • Instant answers to questions from NCERT textbooks.
  • Interactive and user-friendly interface.
  • Covers subjects for classes 9, 10, 11, and 12.
  • Access to study materials, summaries, and explanations.
  • 24/7 availability for students.

How to Use

  1. Open the NCERT Book Chatbot application.
  2. Select your class (9, 10, 11, or 12).
  3. Type your question regarding any topic or subject.
  4. Receive instant responses and explore related materials.
